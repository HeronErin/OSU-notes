Newton I

  • Newton I

An inertial frame of reference is one where an object is not interacting with any other objects experiences zero acceleration.

  • Non-accelerating

Examples:
classroom, car with const speed
Non examples:
Plane taking off, car turning
In an inertial frame of reference an object with no force on it has zero acceleration.
